"My Favorite City: Worcester, Massachusetts"

Worcester, pronounced wustah, is my favorite city because it is so quirky. Rocket ships and the Smiley Face were both invented here. People are not pretentious here. In fact, we are all just trying to survive and we recognize that and enjoy our imperfections.

Worcester has always been a city of immigrants so the population and languages, the food and culture around you is very diverse here. Economically, it is also diverse.

In the last several years, the city has been trying to gentrify Worcester. We now have trendy restaurants, beautiful murals, and some really nice places to walk and enjoy local nature and history.
